How to Use SnapAssist

To search for photography locations for the subject matter of your choice, go to the landing page and type your search keywords into the box. SnapAssist will then search through its database to find photos with matching tags, cluster the photos using their geolocations, and plot the photos and clusters on a map.

On the map, the centre of each cluster is denoted by a blue pin. Clicking the pin provides summary statistics and representative photos (and their EXIF data). The clusters' centres are determined by averaging the longitudes and latitudes of their constituent photos, weighted by the number of views of each photo. The clusters' ranks (#1, above) come from ordering the clusters' average number of views per photo divided by their number of photos, a joint measure of the quality of the photos taken at a location and how original the location is.
